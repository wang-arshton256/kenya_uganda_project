Women & Gender

Areas of Focus

1.Women Settlement Support
    • Information sessions - workshops and webinars
    • Language Support translation of Government documents
    • Women Shelter Establishment
    • Collaboration with Newcomers & Settlement Program
2.Girl Child Empowerment

We empower the girl child through educational programs, mentorship opportunities, and access to resources. We promote leadership skills, encourage participation in sports, and provide workshops to build confidence and raise awareness about their rights.

3.Women Economic Security
    • Uplift women and their children out of poverty and into financial independence
    • Collaborate with Social Economic Empowerment Program initiatives like Biashara Networking Club & Pamoja Bereavement Fund
4.He for She
    • Focuses on men and boys inclusion in women and gender equality activities
    • Collaboration with HeForShe at the United Nations Women and other stakeholders
5.Gender-based Violence
    • Support & counselling for people,& their children, who have experienced violence, that aim to promote violence prevention, or that support systemic change efforts related to gender-based violence.
