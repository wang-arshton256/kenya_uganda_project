Our Values

A vibrant community dedicated to connecting Ugandans living in Canada.

The Foundation of Our Culture

  • 1. Encourage, empower and support community participation
  • 2. Enable people to make their own choices
  • Prioritize those with the highest level of need and vulnerabilities
  • 3. Respect and celebrate diversity and provide services that are inclusive and sensitive
  • 4. Deliver accessible, high quality services in caring, safe environments which create a sense of wellbeing
  • 5. Respond and be accountable to our community and funders
  • 6. Manage resources efficiently and effectively
  • 7. Value and respect staff and volunteers

At the heart of our organization lies a commitment to integrity, innovation, and community. We believe that by staying true to our core values, we not only foster an environment of excellence but also empower our team and enrich the lives of those we serve. Together, we strive to make a positive impact, ensuring our actions reflect our dedication to these principles every day. Join us as we continue to uphold our values and drive meaningful change in our industry and beyond.
