Tourism, Trade and Investment

Driving Economic Growth.

The Ugandan Canadian Association's Tourism, Trade & Investment (TTI) Program is designed to foster collaboration and growth among various stakeholders, including government entities, charitable organizations, and businesses in Canada, Kenya, and other African markets. This initiative recognizes the importance of expanding economic opportunities, cultural exchange, and investment potential in these regions.

Below are some of the areas of focus:

  • Blue Economy Development
  • Food and Agriculture Market Promotion
  • Exports and Supply Chain Management
  • Technology Exchange and Benchmarking
  • Twin Cities Facilitation
  • International Trade Missions
  • Conferences and Exhibitions
  • Foreign Direct Investment Promotion
  • Tourism Promotion
  • G2B and B2B Connections
  • International Travel Advisory and Logistics

KCA aims to work collaboratively with organizations and stakeholders through our grassroots network across the country.
